# Ranowell Hotel — Website Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for the **Ranowell Hotel** website.  
This guide is designed to help you understand the website structure, its pages, and key features.

🔗 **Live Website:** [ranowellhotel.com](https://ranowellhotel.com)

---

## 📌 Website Overview

The Ranowell Hotel website is a modern, mobile-friendly website that showcases the hotel's services, banquet halls, accommodation, weddings, and gallery. It is designed to give guests a premium browsing experience on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop.

---

## 🗂️ Website Pages

### 🏠 Home Page (`/`)
The landing page visitors see first. It includes:
- **Hero banner** with a welcome message
- **Exclusive Offers** — highlights of wedding buffets, reception halls, and hotel rooms
- **Guest Testimonials** — real reviews from couples who celebrated at Ranowell
- **Social Media Feed** — latest posts from the hotel's Facebook page

### 💒 Weddings Page (`/weddings`)
A dedicated page for couples planning their wedding at Ranowell:
- **Wedding video** showcasing past celebrations
- **Featured couples** with links to their full photo albums on Pixieset
- **Banquet hall showcase** — overview of the three available halls

### 🏛️ Banquet Hall Pages
Each banquet hall has its own gallery page with high-quality photos:

| Hall             | Page URL          |
|------------------|-------------------|
| Royal Banquet    | `/royalbanquet`   |
| Kings Court      | `/kingscourt`     |
| Sparkle Lounge   | `/sparklelounge`  |

### 📸 Gallery Page (`/gallery`)
A photo gallery of the hotel with filtering by category:
- **All** — shows every photo
- **Weddings** — wedding ceremony and reception photos
- **Dining** — restaurant and buffet photos
- **Accommodation** — room and suite photos

On mobile devices, the filter appears as a dropdown menu for easy use.

### 🛏️ Accommodation Page (`/accommodation`)
Showcases the hotel's rooms and suites with images, descriptions, and amenities.

### 📋 Fact Sheet Page (`/factsheet`)
A quick-reference page with essential hotel information.

---

## 📱 Mobile-Friendly Design

The website is fully responsive. Every page automatically adjusts its layout for:
- 📱 **Mobile phones** — single-column layout, dropdown menus, optimized images
- 📟 **Tablets** — two-column grids, touch-friendly navigation
- 🖥️ **Desktops** — full multi-column layouts with hover effects and animations

---

## ⚡ Performance

All images across the website have been optimized using the **WebP** format, which loads significantly faster than traditional JPEG or PNG files — ensuring a smooth experience even on slower connections.

## 🛠️ Technical Summary
